## 🧠 The Core Difference (In Plain English)

**Fireflies.ai** records your meeting, then gives you a sophisticated post-meeting report: AI summaries, speaker analytics, sentiment analysis, conversation intelligence, and searchable transcripts. It's the better choice when what happens *after* the meeting matters more — coaching, analysis, pattern-spotting over time.

**Otter.ai** shines *during* the meeting. Its real-time transcription is visible to participants as they speak. Its AI Chat means you can query past meetings like a database the moment they end. It syncs directly with Salesforce and HubSpot. It's built for speed and immediacy.

## 🏆 Verdict by Use Case

### ✅ Choose Fireflies.ai if:
- Your team operates in multiple languages (100+ supported)
- You want post-meeting conversation intelligence and analytics
- You're in sales, recruiting, or any team that reviews meeting data strategically
- You need HIPAA compliance (Enterprise plan)
- You want a customizable AI notetaker bot

### ✅ Choose Otter.ai if:
- You want live transcription visible to all participants during the call
- Your sales team uses HubSpot or Salesforce and needs automatic CRM logging
- You want a more generous, usable free tier to start with
- You operate primarily in English, French, or Spanish
- Fast setup with minimal configuration is important
