# 🧭 Which AI Meeting Tool Should You Choose?
### A Decision Framework: Fireflies.ai vs Otter.ai

Use this checklist to find the right tool based on your actual needs — not just feature lists.

---

## Step 1: Answer These Questions First

Go through each question and tally your answers.

---

### 🌍 Language & Geography

- [ ] **My team primarily speaks English, French, or Spanish** → +1 Otter.ai  
- [ ] **My team uses any other language — or multiple languages** → +1 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **I have international clients on calls in non-English languages** → +1 Fireflies.ai  

---

### ⏱️ When Do You Need the Value?

- [ ] **I need insights immediately during or right after the meeting** → +1 Otter.ai  
- [ ] **I review meetings strategically after the fact** → +1 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **I want to share live captions with all participants as they speak** → +1 Otter.ai  
- [ ] **I want to analyze conversation patterns and coaching data over time** → +1 Fireflies.ai  

---

### 💼 What's Your Team Type?

- [ ] **Sales team — I use HubSpot or Salesforce** → +2 Otter.ai  
- [ ] **Sales team — I want AI coaching and conversation intelligence** → +2 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **Recruiting / HR team** → +1 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **Marketing or content team** → +1 Otter.ai  
- [ ] **Engineering or product team** → neutral (either works)  
- [ ] **Executive / Leadership team** → +1 Fireflies.ai (analytics)  
- [ ] **Educator, journalist, or researcher** → +1 Otter.ai  

---

### 💰 What's Your Budget Situation?

- [ ] **I want to try before committing — free tier matters** → +1 Otter.ai (300 min/month free)  
- [ ] **I'm ready to pay from day one for full features** → neutral  
- [ ] **I'm on a tight budget at Pro tier** → +1 Otter.ai ($8.33 vs $10/seat)  
- [ ] **I need enterprise pricing with compliance guarantees** → +1 Fireflies.ai (HIPAA available)  
- [ ] **I'm a student or teacher** → +1 Otter.ai (20% .edu discount)  

---

### 🏥 Compliance & Security

- [ ] **My organization handles sensitive health/patient data (HIPAA)** → +1 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **GDPR compliance is required** → neutral (both compliant)  
- [ ] **We need SOC 2 compliance** → neutral (both available)  
- [ ] **We need SSO / SAML for Enterprise** → neutral (both offer on Enterprise)  

---

### 🔌 Integration Needs

- [ ] **I need CRM auto-logging into HubSpot natively** → +1 Otter.ai  
- [ ] **I need CRM auto-logging into Salesforce natively** → +1 Otter.ai  
- [ ] **I use dialers like Dialpad, JustCall, or Aircall** → +1 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **I use an ATS (Greenhouse, Lever, Workday) for recruiting** → +1 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **I need Zapier / Make automation support** → neutral (both)  
- [ ] **I want a Chrome extension** → +1 Fireflies.ai  

---

### 🤖 AI Features Priority

- [ ] **I want to "chat" with my meeting notes (ask questions, get instant answers)** → +1 Otter.ai  
- [ ] **I want deep AI summaries with decisions, topics, and sentiment breakdown** → +1 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **I want to see who talked how much and analyze conversation ratios** → +1 Fireflies.ai  
- [ ] **I want the AI to automatically push action items into my CRM** → +1 Otter.ai  

---

## Step 2: Tally Your Score

| Tool | Your Score |
|------|-----------|
| Fireflies.ai | ___ |
| Otter.ai | ___ |

**Higher score = likely better fit for your specific situation.**

---

## Step 3: Sanity Check

Even with a clear score winner, check these dealbreakers:

| Dealbreaker | Tool |
|---|---|
| Need transcription in any language beyond English/French/Spanish | Must use Fireflies.ai |
| Need HIPAA compliance | Must use Fireflies.ai (Enterprise) |
| Need native HubSpot or Salesforce push | Strongly prefer Otter.ai |
| Want live in-meeting captions visible to all | Must use Otter.ai |

---

## Step 4: Test Before You Commit

Both tools have free tiers. There's no reason not to test your actual meeting patterns:

1. **Otter.ai free** — 300 min/month, no credit card needed. Run 2–3 real meetings.
2. **Fireflies.ai free** — Connect it to your calendar. See how the post-meeting summaries look.
3. After one week with each: the right choice will be obvious.
